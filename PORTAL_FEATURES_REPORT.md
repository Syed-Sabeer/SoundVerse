# SingWithMe Platform - Portal Features Summary

## *Admin Panel*

- *Content Management*: Dynamic website content (Home, Contact, Blog, Services), CMS pages, FAQ, Newsletter, Live Videos, News Bar
- *User Management*: Monitor and manage all user accounts, customer list, toggle featured status
- *Artist Management*: Approve/reject artists, manage subscriptions, toggle featured status, view analytics
- *Music Management*: View all songs, toggle featured status, manage artwork uploads
- *Subscription Management*: Create/edit user and artist subscription plans, configure features per plan, monitor active subscriptions
- *Royalty Management*: View royalty calculations, review/approve/process payout requests, view transaction history, generate reports
- *Tip Management*: View all tips, track payment status, view detailed tip information
- *Analytics*: Dashboard metrics, artist analytics, user activity, export reports (CSV/PDF), demographics
- *Services*: Manage music video upload, royalty collection, artist subscription, artwork photo, support networking services
- *Additional*: Chatbot management, partner management, gift/ring orders, ad management, sponsored playlists, system settings

---

## *Artist Portal*

- *Account & Profile*: Registration, profile management, profile customization
- *Music Management*: Upload songs (with metadata), manage music library, edit/delete songs, upload limits based on subscription
- *Artwork Management*: Upload artwork, manage gallery, edit/delete artwork
- *Analytics*: Dashboard, song performance tracking, monthly listeners, demographics, export (CSV), per-track analytics
- *Royalty & Earnings*: Earnings dashboard, payout requests, payout history, transaction history, payment details management, export reports (CSV/PDF), per-track earnings
- *Ad Revenue*: View ad revenue earnings from ad injection system
- *Collaborations*: Create track collaborations, manage ownership splits, approve/reject splits, view revenue distributions
- *Subscription*: View/purchase plans, feature access (unlimited uploads, featured rotation, ISRC codes, certified badge, advanced analytics)

---

## *Customer/User Portal*

- *Account*: Registration, login, account management, profile management
- *Music Experience*: Music discovery, search, full-featured music player, play songs, view song details
- *Playlists*: Create playlists (dedicated page), manage playlists, add/remove songs, playlist limits (free: 3, premium: unlimited), smart playlists
- *Favorites*: Like songs, manage favorites, collections, follow artists, view subscribed artists
- *Subscriptions*: View/purchase plans, feature access:
  - *Free*: Ads during playback, limited playlists (3), no offline downloads, standard quality
  - *Premium*: Ad-free, unlimited playlists, offline downloads, HD audio, tip artists, supporter badge
- *Offline Downloads*: Download songs (premium), manage downloads, download limits
- *Music Interaction*: share songs, follow playlists
- *Statistics*: User stats dashboard (playlists, songs, favorites, subscribed artists), listening history
- *Artist Interaction*: View artist profiles, subscribe to artists, tip artists (premium), browse artist music
- *Payment*: Subscription purchase (Stripe, PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Square), payment processing
- *Additional*: Ad injection (free users), music search API, responsive design, logout
